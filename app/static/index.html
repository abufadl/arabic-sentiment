Arabic Sentiment Classification التحليل العاطفي للنصوص العربية

Using this neural nets model (MULTIFiT), you can classify Arabic reviews or similar text as positive or negative.
Neutral texts are not predicted as such (only positive and negative classes).
Try not to use too much dialect or slang in your text. The model does not handle that.
Arabic diacritics and Non-Arabic text will be ignored.
